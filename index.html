Our Story

At Vogue, we believe that fragrance is more than a scent—it’s an experience. Inspired by timeless elegance and modern sophistication, each perfume is carefully crafted with the finest ingredients to tell a story unique to you. From the first spritz to the lingering base notes, our collection embodies grace, passion, and artistry.

Crafted for the Senses, Designed for the Soul

Signature Scents

Velour Essence $250

A sophisticated blend of amber, vanilla, and sandalwood, Velour Essence wraps you in warmth and elegance. Perfect for evening wear, it leaves a lasting impression of luxury.

Luna Noire$250

Inspired by the mystery of the night, Luna Noire combines jasmine, blackcurrant, and musk for a bold yet graceful fragrance. It’s the perfect balance of sensuality and charm.

Fresh and radiant, Étoile Blanche features citrus, white florals, and cedarwood. A fragrance that captures the purity of starlight, ideal for daily wear with a modern touch.
